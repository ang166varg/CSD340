Kevin: That’s my job — that’s what I do.
Milton: Exactly. And that’s why _________ is my favorite sin. Self-love, pride, ambition — the easiest, most addictive drug. Better to reign in Hell than serve in Heaven.

Milton: No, I don’t judge anyone. As far as I’m concerned, everyone’s a _________. God’s the trickster. He gives man this extraordinary gift — _________ — and then writes rules that forbid their use. Look but don’t touch, taste but don’t swallow — while you struggle, he laughs. Worship that? Never.
